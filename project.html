Body. When the management company of 'Moscow city' sought to change the way people perceive the biggest commercial district in Russia we approached to assist in developing a new experience and a new attitude towards the place.

The outcome was the transformation of the space that led to a new experience both for those who work within the business center and for those who wished to visit and feel the real spirit of business acumen and innovation behind the brand that we developed.
